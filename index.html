Biology

The study of living organisms, divided into many specialized fields that cover their morphology, physiology, anatomy, behavior, origin, and distribution.

Chemistry

The identification of the substances of which matter is composed; the investigation of their properties and the ways in which they interact, combine, and change; and the use of these processes to form new substances.

About

Where did you get these notes?

I took these notes while taking biology and chemistry honors myself. Much of the information is based on Google, Holt California Biology Textbook, and Modern Chemistry Textbook. I claim no responsibility for inaccurate info, please don't sue if you get a bad score on the SAT Bio/Chem.

I'm taking the SAT Bio/Chem subject test. How do I prepare?

The Barron's SAT books, plus this website, holds nearly everything you'd need to prepare for the test. Make sure to take at least 3-4 practice tests before the actual test, and thoroughly read the Barron's book. Unless you still feel mightily unprepared, you probably don't need any other books.

I'm looking for chemistry practice questions. Where can I find them?

I recommend chemteam.info, which also happens to have an excellent humor section.
